    16:15 = s (Semitone)
    10:9 = t (Minor tone)
    9:8 = T (Major tone)

which combine to form:

    6:5 = Ts (minor third)
    5:4 = Tt (major third)
    4:3 = Tts (perfect fourth)
    3:2 = TTts (perfect fifth)
    2:1 = TTTttss (octave)
